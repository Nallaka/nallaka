About Me

I am a second year pre-med student studying Biochemistry and Computer Science at the University of Alabama. The intersection of technology and healthcare is passion of mine, and I hope to explore novel applications of growing technologies in the healthcare field through a doctoral degree.

My current interests involve emerging machine learning technologies as well as the application of deep learning and energy-based models for medical image processing.
